“Hi \[Customer Name\], it’s \[Your Name\] calling from Bright Car Finance.

I wanted to give you a quick courtesy call regarding the finance application you made yesterday, is now a good time to chat?”

**If yes:**

“Perfect. The good news is your finance has already been approved and we’ve verified your income, so we’re nearly there. The only thing we need to get things moving is a copy of your driving licence.”

**Qualifying Questions (keep it vague & open-ended)**

* “Just so I can point you in the right direction, what type of vehicle are you thinking of? Something smaller and economical, or maybe something a bit bigger and more practical?”  
* “Are you mainly looking for something to get around town, or more for longer trips and family use?”  
* “Do you have a preferred style in mind, for example hatchback, SUV, or something sportier?”  
* “Is fuel efficiency important to you, or are you looking more for comfort and space?”

*(Designed to be of assistance to the customer, not pushy or over qualifying.)*

**Budget questions**

“How much are you happy to pay each month for the new vehicle?” 

- If someone says “I don’t know”, we use “no problem, are we talking £200/£300 a month or £400/£500 a month?”
